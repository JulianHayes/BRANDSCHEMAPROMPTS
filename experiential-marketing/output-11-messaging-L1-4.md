## Layer 1: Master Narrative

Every piece of Fisher XP communication flows from this narrative. It is the single source of truth for what the company says and why it matters.

---

The experiential marketing industry has a structural problem. The agencies that deliver the most ambitious brand experiences cannot navigate the regulatory environment that governs them. And the firms that understand regulation cannot create anything worth attending.

This gap is widening. The European Accessibility Act is now enforceable. The EU AI Act's high-risk provisions take effect in August 2026. CSRD mandates Scope 3 disclosure that includes event procurement. The UK's Data Use and Access Act has raised PECR fines to £17.5 million. And the Digital Markets, Competition and Consumers Act means sustainability claims at events must now be substantiated or face penalties of up to 10% of global turnover.

Premium brands in regulated industries — financial services, insurance, wealth management, B2B technology — face an impossible choice: hire a creative agency that produces remarkable experiences but treats compliance as an afterthought, or hire a compliance consultancy that produces functional events nobody remembers.

Fisher XP exists because that choice is false.

We design brand experiences where compliance is a creative input, not a legal constraint. Where measurement is instrumented from concept stage, not retrofitted after the event. Where the experience is ambitious precisely because the regulatory framework is already resolved.

Founded by Justin Fisher and Julian Hayes — with a combined 20+ years at American Express, Aviva, and Alter Domus — Fisher XP brings together experiential delivery, brand governance, and regulatory fluency in a single practice. We do not outsource compliance to your legal team. We do not treat measurement as a wrap report. We build both into the architecture of every experience we design.

The result: experiences that are bolder because they are built on solid ground. Proof points that survive CFO scrutiny. And a compliance position that protects your brand before, during, and after the activation.

What we learned is that the best brand experiences in regulated environments come from integrating compliance and creativity at the concept stage — not running them as separate, sequential processes.

Fisher XP does three things differently. First, compliance is a creative input. We brief our creative process against the regulatory framework, so concepts survive legal review first time. Second, measurement is instrumented from the start. We don't count footfall — we attribute pipeline. Third, our pricing is transparent. Fixed strategy fees, open-book production costs, no hidden mark-ups.

We work with financial services firms, insurers, wealth managers, and B2B technology companies. Our entry point is a Compliance Readiness Assessment — a £5K-£8K diagnostic that maps your experiential programme against current regulations and identifies exactly where you are exposed. ## Layer 3: Audience-Specific Messaging

### For the CMO or Head of Experience

Your experiential programme should be your most powerful brand asset. Instead, it is constrained by a compliance process that kills ambition and a measurement system that cannot prove value.

Fisher XP reverses both problems. We design experiences where compliance is resolved before creative begins — so your team stops losing three weeks to legal review cycles. And we instrument measurement into the experience architecture, so you can demonstrate pipeline impact to your CFO within 10 working days of the activation.

The result: you present bolder work to your board, backed by evidence that justifies the investment. Your programme grows instead of being questioned.

### For the Head of Digital / Head of Events

You need a partner who understands your technology stack, your data requirements, and your operational constraints — not another agency that hands you a creative concept and expects you to figure out the implementation.

Fisher XP's measurement framework integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Cvent, and Tableau. Our compliance framework produces documentation your legal and IT security teams will accept. And our delivery model is designed to work alongside your internal team, not replace them.

We provide the strategic architecture. You retain operational control.

### For Procurement

Fisher XP operates on a transparent commercial model. Strategy and management fees are fixed and agreed upfront. Production costs are passed through at cost plus a disclosed handling fee of 15-20%. Open-book pricing is available on request.

We hold professional indemnity insurance at £[X]M, public liability at £[X]M, and Cyber Essentials certification. Our standard terms include clear scope definitions, change order protocols, and defined deliverables with acceptance criteria.

Our Tier 1 diagnostics (£5K-£8K) fall within most discretionary approval limits, allowing you to evaluate our methodology before committing to a larger engagement.

### For the CFO

Experiential marketing budgets are under scrutiny because measurement is inadequate. Most agencies report attendance, social impressions, and Net Promoter Scores — metrics that prove nothing about commercial impact.

Fisher XP's measurement framework attributes specific pipeline and revenue outcomes to each activation. You receive a post-event attribution report within 10 working days — not a wrap document three weeks later showing how many people walked through the door.

Our diagnostic service (£5K-£8K) audits your current measurement approach and designs a framework tied to the business metrics you already track. The cost is recovered if it prevents one budget cycle of unsubstantiated experiential spend.

### For the Agency Partner

Fisher XP is designed to complement, not replace. We provide a strategic compliance and measurement layer that sits on top of your production capability.

For lead agencies: we make your work more defensible. Concepts developed with our compliance framework survive client legal review faster, reducing revision cycles and protecting your creative reputation.

For specialist agencies: we extend your capability into regulated verticals where governance requirements currently exclude you from pitching.

Our commercial model for agency partnerships is a fixed strategy fee, agreed per project, with clear scope boundaries. We do not compete for your production revenue.

## Layer 4: Sector-Specific Angles

### Financial Services

FCA financial promotions rules, Consumer Duty requirements, and the new PECR fine regime create a compliance surface that most experiential agencies cannot navigate. Your investor days, adviser conferences, and client events must be memorable and compliant — and the penalties for getting it wrong have increased by a factor of 35.

Fisher XP was built for this environment. Both founders spent years creating premium brand experiences within FCA-regulated businesses. We understand the difference between a financial promotion and a brand communication — and we design experiences that are ambitious within those boundaries, not constrained by them.

### Insurance

Insurance events face unique regulatory pressures: IDD compliance for adviser-facing activations, Consumer Duty requirements for policyholder engagement, and GDPR/DUAA obligations for data capture at scale. Most experiential agencies treat these as your compliance team's problem.

Fisher XP builds regulatory requirements into the creative brief. The result: concepts that arrive at your compliance committee already framed within regulatory boundaries. Fewer revision cycles. Faster approval. Bolder events.

### Wealth Management

High-net-worth client events are your most direct relationship touchpoint. They must reflect the premium positioning of your brand while meeting FCA, GDPR, and financial promotions requirements that govern every interaction.

Fisher XP designs wealth management experiences where exclusivity, discretion, and compliance coexist. Our measurement framework tracks relationship deepening and AUM conversion — not vanity metrics — giving your client-facing teams evidence that the programme drives commercial outcomes.

### B2B Technology

Your buyers research solutions through AI assistants, peer communities, and conference attendance before they ever speak to sales. Experiential is becoming the proof point that digital channels cannot replicate — but only if it is measured, attributed, and connected to your CRM pipeline.

Fisher XP's measurement framework attributes leads from events to pipeline within 10 working days. We integrate with Salesforce, HubSpot, and Marketo — not as an afterthought, but as part of the experience architecture. Your events team gets creative ambition. Your sales team gets attributed leads. Your CFO gets ROI evidence.

### Professional Services (Law Firms, Accountancies, Consultancies)

Client events are relationship infrastructure — but they are rarely measured and frequently formulaic. The annual partner dinner, the client seminar, the thought leadership breakfast: these formats have not evolved in a decade, and attendance is declining.

Fisher XP redesigns professional services experiential programmes to deliver measurable client engagement, demonstrate thought leadership through experience design rather than PowerPoint, and produce content assets that extend the value of every activation beyond the day itself.

### Private Equity and Asset Management

Investor days and LP meetings are high-stakes, low-margin-for-error events. The audience is sophisticated, time-poor, and sceptical of anything that looks like marketing. Every element must demonstrate operational competence and strategic clarity.

Fisher XP designs investor experiences that communicate conviction through environment, narrative, and interaction design — not slide decks. Our compliance framework ensures AIFMD, FCA, and financial promotions requirements are met without reducing the experience to a regulated communications exercise.
